Welcome to my site!

I study what kind of cognitive system allows people to make moral judgments the way we do. Currently, I am doing a PhD in linguistics, supervised by Prof. Lian-Hee WEE at Hong Kong Baptist University. My dissertation project explores whether grammatical frameworks adequately explain how people make moral judgments. My wider interests include eye-tracking, prosody, and orthography.

I completed an M.A. in Philosophy at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (2022). I edit a student-led journal FLaP FIRST: Journal for Formal Linguistics and Philosophy (ISSN: 3105-6415). I am an Associate Fellow of AdvanceHE (AFHEA).
